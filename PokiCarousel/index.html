Cubone is a bipedal Pokémon with a light brown hide and a short tail. Its eyes are large and red, often conveying a sense of sadness or loneliness. Despite its tough exterior, Cubone is said to be quite emotional and sensitive, mourning the loss of its mother throughout its life.

Gengar is a dark purple, ghost-type Pokémon with a gaseous body. It has a mischievous grin, large, pointed ears, and glowing red eyes that add to its sinister look. Gengar's body is said to be made of a shadowy substance, allowing it to slip through walls and even absorb surrounding heat, causing a noticeable chill in the air.
